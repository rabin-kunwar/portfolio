About

I am currently pursuing the Master's Degree Programme in Forestry (MDP FOR) at the University of Eastern Finland (UEF), School of Forest Science, Joensuu. Previously, I have worked as a research assistant for Tribhuvan University (T.U), Institute of Forestry (IOF), Office of the Dean, Kathmandu. I completed the Bachelor of Science in Forestry (B.Sc. Forestry) from T.U, IOF, Hetauda campus. My research interests exclusively lie in forest resources assessment, digitalization, and wood procurement. I also have built an allometric biomass equation for one of the most common shrub species in Nepal.

I am now focusing on forest planning, geoinformatics, bioeconomy, ecosystem visioning, wildfires, invasive species outbreaks, and land cover change detection, integrating human-nature relationships. Also, I am familiar with statistical data analysis, mapping, and scientific writing. I am working on a digital platform to equip teachers and students with a digital tool to enhance research, dissertations, and classroom teaching in the field of forest and range science.
